Why work at North Bay Glass

  • Real commercial work — Curtain wall, storefronts, and rated glazing on landmark Bay Area projects — not just repairs.
  • A certified, growing firm — Licensed, bonded, DIR-registered, and SBE/LBE certified, with a steady pipeline of public and commercial work.
  • Craftsmanship & safety first — We invest in training, equipment, and a safety-first culture on every site.
  • Room to grow — We develop our staff and create room to take on more responsibility as the company expands.
