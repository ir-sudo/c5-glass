About us

Your vision, crafted to perfection.

At North Bay Glass, we turn architectural visions into reality — leveraging our expertise, engineering support, and precision to deliver glazing that meets the highest standards on every commercial, institutional, and government project.

Based in San Francisco and serving the Bay Area for over a decade, we design, fabricate, and install curtain wall, storefronts, entrances, and architectural, structural, and security glazing. We're licensed, bonded, and insured (CSLB #000000) and certified for public works — DIR PW-LR-1001102510, SBE #2035232, and SF LBE #CMD02251000602 — with a track record on federal, state, and civic buildings.

We also serve residential clients with windows, glass, tinting, and 24/7 emergency board-up. Whatever the project, we offer free estimates and won't leave until you're satisfied with the results.
