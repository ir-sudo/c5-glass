Certified contractor

Certified Public-Works & Government Glazing

San Francisco's certified small-business glazing contractor for public, institutional, and government projects — DIR-registered, SBE, and SF LBE.

Public and institutional projects can't use just any glazier. They require a DIR-registered contractor for prevailing-wage work, and they reward agencies for using certified small and local businesses. North Bay Glass is built for exactly this work — and we have the federal, state, and civic track record to prove it.

From storefronts and curtain wall to interior partitions, decorative film, and ongoing glass maintenance, we deliver certified, compliant glazing for general contractors and public agencies across the Bay Area.

Why choose them

Why agencies & GCs choose North Bay Glass

  • DIR registered — Public Works contractor registration (PW-LR-1001102510) — eligible to bid and perform prevailing-wage public work.
  • SBE certified — State Small Business Enterprise (#2035232) — helps agencies meet small-business participation goals.
  • SF LBE certified — San Francisco Local Business Enterprise (CMD #CMD02251000602) — earns bid discounts and credits on City & County contracts.
  • Licensed, bonded & insured — CSLB C-17 glazing contractor, license #000000 — fully bonded and insured.
  • Proven on government work — Federal Buildings in SF & Oakland, State (DGS) buildings, and ongoing maintenance contracts.
  • Architectural & engineering support — Dedicated teams for curtain wall and storefront installations on demanding, code-driven projects.
